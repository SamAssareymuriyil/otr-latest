Our Team

Our team consists of three notable departments, commercial, manufacturing and technical departments. From there, they branch off into sub-departments. The goal of this is to expose new students to real-life engineering experiences so that they can be better prepared for the challenges of tomorrow. We strive to keep a multi-disciplinary team of engineers, business and marketing students and expose them to fields of knowledge beyond the classrooms.

Ontario Tech Racing

We are a team based out of the Faculty of Engineering & Applied Science at Ontario Tech University that empowers students with the enriching, hands-on learning experience of designing, budgeting, and building a electric race car. We are a student design team that cultivates innovation and embraces the multidisciplinary challenges faced by professional motorsport teams.
