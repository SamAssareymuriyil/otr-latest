This is Our Classroom

Ontario Tech Racing is a club based out of the Faculty of Engineering & Applied Science at Ontario Tech University that empowers students with the enriching, hands-on learning experience of designing, budgeting, and building a Formula-style electric race car. We are a student design team that cultivates innovation and embraces the multidisciplinary challenges faced by professional motorsport teams.


We are dedicated to researching and developing the future of automotive propulsion technologies, with the backing of our faculty and sponsors. We place value and emphasis on teamwork and efficiency as well as the art of knowledge transfer here at Ontario Tech Racing.

About The Competiton

Formula SAE is a student design competition organized by SAE International. The goal is for students to develop a small Formula-style racecar that is to be evaluated for its potential as a production item. The racecar has to adhere to the non-professional autocross weekend racer.

Acceleration

Measures the vehicle's acceleration over a 75m straight track

Endurance

Measures the vehicle's durability and reliability over a 22km race

Autocross

Measures the vehicle's maneuverability and handling over a tight sprint course with a series of turns and straight paths

Efficiency

Measures the vehicle's energy efficiency during the endurance event

Skid-Pad

Measures the vehicle's cornering ability over a figure-8 with constant radius

Design

Evaluation of design methods used during the design, testing, integration, and validation phases of every component

Sales Presentation

Evaluates each team's business case which argues that their vehicle is best for the active racing market

Cost Report

Evaluates the team's ability to produce and solve problems under an efficient budget while remaining competitive
